A Dream at Bus Stop

by Yeqian Li

I think that the display window of the collaborative venture between COACH and the artist Basquiat could not express the theme of the event well, so I decide to design a better one for it. I made a stop-motion animation for the project, telling the story of Basquiat bringing color and happiness to a young man's dull life.

This animation is composed of 61 digital illustrations created through Procreate. My painting style imitated Basquiat's street graffiti style to make the work look more harmonious. It is also a tribute to Basquiat.
